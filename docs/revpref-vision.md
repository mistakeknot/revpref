# RevPref Vision

RevPref helps people understand their computers through revealed preference: what they actually use, what runs without attention, and what deserves review.

The product should feel like a calm maintenance desk, not a panic-inducing cleaner. It should say, "Here is the evidence; here are your options; nothing happens unless you choose it."

## Product Promise

- Local app usage review without cloud telemetry.
- Evidence-first recommendations.
- Startup and uninstall review without automatic removal.
- Cross-platform app metadata and collectors that are understandable enough to audit.

## First Useful Moment

A user runs RevPref and sees a ranked list like:

- "EA App: not opened in 130 days, starts with Windows, background runtime observed."
- "Large Video Editor: not opened in 193 days, uses 24 GiB."
- "Discord: used daily, keep."

The user can mark apps as keep, disable startup outside RevPref, or open the official uninstall path.
